ARAD (In Progress)

The Atlantic Research Article Database, or ARAD for short is a project by the Dalhousie Pscychology Department to create an Article database which allows users to review articles based on perceived difficulty and reading time, and to leave comments and general feedback on the papers.

ARAD is supposed to make the selection of articles for Pschycology units easier by allowing professors to gauge previous student responses to papers.
